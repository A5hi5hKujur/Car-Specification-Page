Overview

The Vios’ rival in the B-segment market and recently with it’s new design and colour, the Honda City has become very desirable because at the price range, it hits a sweet spot amongst the populace here in Malaysia. Reliable as ever, the Honda City offers stability control and 6 airbags (for the highest spec model) which gives it the edge. Rear legroom has been a plus for the previous generations of City and this model doesn’t disappoint. Boot space is huge and definitely becomes a good plus points for family trips. Honda Malaysia has removed the ‘Brilliant Sporty Blue Metallic’ color option and replaced with a more striking ‘Dark Ruby Red Pearl’.

+ Fantastic design

+ Ample space in the car

+ Nimble and spritely in the city

+ Fuel economical

- Poor NVH

- Engine noise can be easily heard inside the car
